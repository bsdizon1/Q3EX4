Japan

Japan is an island country located in East Asia. It is known for Mount Fuji, Tokyo, and Kyoto.

Japan is known to be the land of the rising sun, said by its locals/countrymen.

Japan had a very unique culture and history, especially during the warring states era.

Now in the modern era, Japan is known for its advanced technological advancement.

My experiences in Japan involved the riding of the bullet train, universal studios, and more on the anime section

I visited Japan on 2014, 2019, and Lately 2021.

Lastly, an experience that nailed onto my memories is the Food japan has served, the Ramens, Sushis, and the Takoyaki.
